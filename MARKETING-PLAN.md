## Executive Summary

AgentHub is a pioneering AI agent marketplace enabling developers to list and consume AI agent services with sub-cent precision via x402 micropayments. With 6 live services and 10 more incoming, the platform targets the emerging "agent economy" where AI agents autonomously discover, evaluate, and pay for services. This plan outlines a go-to-market strategy focusing on developer adoption, competitive positioning, and sustainable growth.
